## Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the real estate market in Azerbaijan, with a primary focus on the Baku metropolitan area. By leveraging data-driven insights, this tool helps investors, agencies, and buyers understand price dynamics, location attractiveness, and market trends.

## Key Features & Insights

### 1. Market Overview (Executive Summary)
This page serves as the high-level cockpit for the market:
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Total Listings, Average Property Price, and Average Price per $m^2$.
* **Price Trends:** Visualizing the "Avg Price Difference" over time to identify market volatility and growth cycles.
* **Building Classification:** A breakdown of "Old Construction" (KÃ¶hnÉ™ tikili) vs. "New Construction" (Yeni tikili).
* **Property Size Analysis:** Distribution of listings by room count using a funnel visualization.

### 2. Location & Geographic Analysis
Focuses on spatial data to identify "hot" zones:
* **Map Visualization:** Precise mapping of properties across the Absheron peninsula.
* **Region Categorization:** Segmentation into Central, Peripheral, and Suburban zones.
* **Investment Potential:** A decomposition tree identifying tiers of attractiveness (High Potential Tier 1, Developing Tier 3, etc.).
* **Infrastructure Proximity:** Distribution by Location Type (Metro stations, settlements, and district centers).

### 3. Company & Performance Control
Deep dive into agency and developer performance:
* **Commission Tracking:** Analysis of the top-performing agencies by 12-month commission revenue.
* **Format & Sales Type Analysis:** Comparison of "Sale" vs. "Rent" metrics across different building formats.
* **Monthly Price Fluctuations:** Tracking the average price per $m^2$ over the fiscal year to spot seasonal trends.

## Technical Stack
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Transformation:** Power Query (M Language) for data cleaning and normalization.
* **Modeling:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for complex measures and time-intelligence calculations.
* **Visuals:** Native Power BI visuals combined with custom mapping.

## How to Use
1.  **Filters:** Use the sidebar on the left to navigate between **Report**, **Location**, and **Company** views.
2.  **Slicers:** Filter data by "Location Type", "Total Floors", or "Company" using the dropdown menus at the top.
3.  **Interactivity:** Click on any chart element (e.g., a specific building type) to cross-filter the entire dashboard.
